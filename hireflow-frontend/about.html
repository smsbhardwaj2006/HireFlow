About HireFlow

HireFlow is a College Placement Management System built to replace manual, spreadsheet-driven campus recruitment with a single, role-based platform. Students, recruiting companies, and placement officers each get a dedicated portal designed around what they actually need to do.

The goal is to remove the friction points that slow placements down: duplicated data entry, lost track of who applied where, manual interview scheduling over email, and placement statistics compiled by hand at the end of the season.

This is a portfolio project demonstrating full-stack development with a Django REST backend and a role-aware frontend — built to be presented and discussed in technical interviews, not a production deployment for a real institution.
